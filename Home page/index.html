About This App and Developer

Built by a Student, Powered by Passion

Streaks wasn't born in a corporate office or a fancy development studio. It was built by Tafazzul Ahmed Mahim, a college student who simply wanted to build something useful — and did exactly that. What started as a hobby project turned into a fully functional Android app, designed and developed entirely in his spare time between classes and assignments. No big team, no budget, no deadlines — just a love for coding and a desire to create something that actually helps people in their daily lives.

What is Streaks?

Streaks is your personal health and productivity companion — a free Android app designed to help you build habits, track daily tasks, and stay consistent with your goals. Here's what makes Streaks great:
    1. 🔥 Streak Tracking — Stay motivated by keeping your daily streaks alive. Missing a day feels real, which makes showing up feel rewarding.
    2. ✅ Daily Task Management — Organize your day with a clean, simple task list that doesn't overwhelm you.
    3. 💪 Health Habit Support — Whether it's drinking water, exercising, or sleeping on time, Streaks helps you stay on track.
    4. 🎯 Simple & Focused — No bloat, no subscriptions, no unnecessary features. Just what you need to stay consistent.
    5. 📱 Lightweight — Runs smoothly on Android 7.0 and above, even on older devices.
    6. 🆓 Completely Free — No hidden costs, no ads interrupting your flow. Built for people, not profit.

Why Streaks?

Most productivity apps are either too complex or hidden behind paywalls. Streaks was made with one goal in mind — to be genuinely useful to everyday people, especially students and young adults trying to build better habits. It's proof that great things don't always come from big companies. Sometimes they come from a college student with a laptop, a vision, and a whole lot of free time.
